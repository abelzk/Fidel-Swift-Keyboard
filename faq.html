Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered here

How does Fidel Swift work?

Fidel Swift listens to your keystrokes and instantly converts phonetic Latin input into accurate Amharic syllables using Unicode characters. It works in real-time across all Windows apps without requiring font installation or language changes.

Do I need to install any fonts or enable Amharic in Windows?

No. Fidel Swift outputs standard Unicode Amharic characters. As long as you're using a modern font that supports Ethiopic script (like Nyala or Abyssinica SIL), Amharic will display correctly without any additional setup.

Is Fidel Swift free to use?

Yes! Fidel Swift is free for personal and professional use. Future versions may introduce optional premium features, but the core typing functionality will remain free.

Is my keystroke data being recorded?

No. Fidel Swift does not store or transmit any keystroke data. It operates locally and only transforms characters in real-time—it does not log, save, or share anything you type.
